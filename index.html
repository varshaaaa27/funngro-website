About Us

Funngro is a mobile app that helps teenagers and young adults earn money and build real-world skills by completing projects for businesses. Users can accept tasks in areas like marketing, content creation, and app testing, gaining work experience and income while they learn. The platform connects young talent with companies seeking assistance on practical tasks.
